Organic fertilisers

We also offer Organic fertilisers

Organic fertilizers are derived from natural sources, such as plant and animal materials. They provide essential nutrients to plants while improving soil health and promoting sustainable farming practices.

Organic fertilizers are typically slower to release nutrients compared to synthetic fertilizers, making them ideal for long-term soil health and fertility.

They can enhance soil structure, water retention, and microbial activity, leading to healthier plants and improved crop yields.

Organic fertilizers are often used in organic farming and gardening, where chemical inputs are minimized or avoided.
